General Information

We aim to introduce early career researchers (faculty and postdocs) to the foundation for competing in the fierce world of professional research by identifying and obtaining funding.

This two-day research workshop, hosted by Computational Science and Engineering at the University of Illinois, will introduce researchers to grantsmanship, the art of writing proposals and acquiring research funding.

  • Day 1 (Saturday November 8)—Grantsmanship Workshop
  • Day 2 (Sunday November 9)—Strategic Grant Development Workshop

      • Participants should bring their own laptops to work on—contact Neal Davis if you need special accomodation.
